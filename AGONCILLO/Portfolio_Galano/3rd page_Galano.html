Lesson 1:Types of Computers

A computer is a device that can be programmed to manipulate symbols.It replies to a specific set of instructions and can perform a pre-recorded list of instructions, say a program. Also, it can instantly store and recover large amounts of data. Computers are primaly arranged according to purpose, size, and data handling functionalities.

There are different types of computer technology available these days. Let us understand the different typees of computers one by one:

1. Micro Computers
2. Mini Computer
3. Mainframe Computer
4. Workstation
5. PC (Personal Computer)

And on the basis of data handling capabilities, there are three types of computer:

1. Analogue Computer
2. Digital Computer
3. Hybrid Computer
